Nigerian Okro Soup

Description

Okra Soup is one of the quickest and easiest Nigerian soups to prepare. Some argue that a lot of work goes to the cutting of the two vegetables used in preparing this recipe. Yes but once the vegetables are ready; it cooks in no time at all.

Ingredients

  • Fresh okra, diced or grated
  • Fresh ugu/pumpkin leaves (spinach as substitute)
  • Ground hot pepper
  • Beef, cut in bits
  • Soft cow skin, cut in bits
  • prawns
  • Smoked fish
  • Dried fish
  • Periwinkle
  • Ground crayfish
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef in water with onion slices, seasoning cubes and salt till it is done. Then add the cow skin/ponmo to the pot and top up with some water. Bring to boil.
  2. Add the crayfish then the smoked fish and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the pepper and periwinkles, cook for some minutes. Add seasoning cubes and adjust salt to taste.
  4. Then add prawns, cover pot and allow to simmer for about 5 minutes.
  5. Now add diced Okro and vegetables, stir well. Cover pot and allow to simmer.
  6. Serve with eba (garri), or swallow of choice.
  7. Enjoy!
